How It Works

The Suraag
behind the magic

The name Suraag means "clue" in Hindi. And that's exactly what our tech does — it finds a way to keep your music alive, even when the signal leaves no trace.

1
Stream Normally Online
While you're connected, Suraag streams your music in real-time at the best quality your network allows.
2
Smart Background Caching
Behind the scenes, Suraag quietly caches the current and upcoming tracks into a smart local buffer.
3
Network Drop Detected
The moment your signal weakens or disappears, Suraag detects it instantly and switches to local cache.
4
Music Never Stops
You hear nothing different. The song keeps playing. No buffering. No notification. Just music — always.
